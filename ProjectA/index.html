Fishursula

Scientific Name:

the name of my creature is Fishursula

Discovered by/at:

Cynthia Liu, Fall 2024

Family:

Belongs to the Diodontidae fish family.
There are plenty of them in the world!

Habitat:

any body of water

Appearance:

A cute little fish that is just a bit shy to see you! Taking the shape of a traditional fish, it is special by its color & size-changing body.

Key Characteristics:

  • Fluctuates in size
  • Color-changing
  • Loves *your* worms

What we know so far about this creature: Despite there being two fishing rods, each time you try to feed it, it will come towards you and choose your bait
(that other one didn't look that good anyways...)
When there is only the gold bait available, it would rather swim around, seemingly always waiting for something... or someone?

Guidelines for human interaction with this creature: Click anywhere in the water to feed fishursula, it will go to wherever your mouse is. However, make sure to still stay so it can get your bait! Press "r" on your keyboard to regenerate a worm! At the same time, Fishursula will change to a whole new fish (change color and size).
